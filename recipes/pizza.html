Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (about 110째F / 45째C)
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for brushing)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water and sugar. Sprinkle the yeast over the water and let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, olive oil, and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture.
  3. Stir the mixture until a dough forms. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour as needed.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it in the oven.
  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions. Roll out each portion into a round pizza crust about 12 inches in diameter.
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a pizza pan or a parchment paper-lined pizza stone. Brush the crust with olive oil.
  8. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, then add any additional toppings like pepperoni or basil.
  10. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  11. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot!
